Artificial AI v2.0

The first Artificial AI that my co-developer and I started almost three years ago — project Ada — is coming along nicely. Her (non-computer) vision, (non-machine) learning, NLP, and planning systems are coming along nicely.

Here’s a recent screenshot1.

Ada (AAI v1.0)

Encouraged by the success of the first version of the project we decided to take the plunge and start on version 2.

We call this version “Edith Valerie Reid”.

Edith (AAI v2.0)

Her initial release on the 26th of July and so far everything is going really well. As with the first version, there are some issues to resolve such as the sleep-synchronisation routines but these are very minor and, from what I’ve heard, common to this type of project.

All in all, I’m declaring the version two release a resounding success.
